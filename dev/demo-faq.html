Welcome
Veterans

The U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Veterans Benefits Administration (VBA) and the Association of Military Banks of America (AMBA) entered into an agreement to create banking options for Veterans and their families through this partnership.

Frequently Asked Questions for Beneficiaries

Neither VA nor AMBA is endorsing any potential bank referrals or requiring Veterans and other beneficiaries to use them. The referrals will be strictly construed and understood to provide additional avenues for fiscal tools that eligible Veterans will be free to explore or ignore.

The Association of Military Banks of America (AMBA) will provide Veterans with increased banking options, support, and access to education necessary for them to achieve greater financial independence, resiliency, and literacy, which ultimately is in the best interest of Veterans. Prociding more manking options for our Veterans and other beneficiaries is important for Veterans and benficiaries to have an additional secure way to electronically receive their VA benefits. They will also assist VA beneficiaries in managing their monetary benefits, in order to reduce the number of fraud cases reported to VA.

The partnership with AMBA member bans and financial institutions affords certain Veterans with potential access to banks and finiacial institutions that they previously may otherwise not have access to, based on their credit and/or legal history.

This partnership will provide eligible Veterans and beneficiraies with an opportunity to open a bank account with the partner banks an receive their funding electronically. Furthermore, it will give our customers the opportunity to receive other benfits from banks that otherwise are not available.
